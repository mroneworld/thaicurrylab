Our Mission

More Than a Curry Kit

Every Thai Curry Lab purchase connects you directly to Thai farming families, traditional food heritage, and the communities who have grown these ingredients for generations.

Thai Farming Families

Ingredients purchased directly from rural Thai farmers, cutting out wholesale intermediaries and putting more income where it matters.

Preserving Heritage

We work with farmers growing heirloom Thai herbs and spice varieties that risk being displaced by industrial agriculture.

Rural Communities

Every kit creates sustainable additional income streams for farming communities in Thailand's herb and spice-growing regions.

Sustainable Farming

Our supply chain prioritises small-batch, low-chemical farming aligned with traditional Thai agricultural practices.

Thai Heritage Globally

Each kit carries a QR-linked Farm-to-Kitchen story — bringing the origins of Thai cuisine to kitchens around the world.

What's Inside

Everything You Need to Cook Like a Thai Chef

Every kit contains six carefully considered components — designed to deliver a restaurant-quality result and a genuinely interactive cooking experience.

  • Authentic Thai curry base — dried, shelf-stable, sourced from Thai farms
  • Aroma Enhancement Pack — galangal, kaffir lime leaf, Thai basil
  • Heat Enhancement Pack — three chilli intensity levels
  • Richness Enhancement Pack — coconut cream powder, pandan
  • Step-by-Step Recipe Guide — bilingual, with Thai food culture notes
  • QR Video Tutorial — 5-min Thai chef demo, scan and cook along
